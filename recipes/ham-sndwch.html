Ham Sandwich

A great snack for a packed lunch or to eat right away!

Ingredients

  • Tomatos: Two slices of a fresh tomato should do the trick
  • Mayonaise: At least two table spoons for spread
  • Lettuce: I personally prefer iceburg, but roman is good as well. If you really wanna be boujie go with butter lettuce
  • Ham: This one is all up two preference and what your butcher has. When they slice it ask for it to be paper thin
  • Bread: I like sourdough personally but up to you
  • Bacon(Optional): Any Canadian applewood smoked bacon is amazing
